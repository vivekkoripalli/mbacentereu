Short Trip to Front-End

Always dreamed of learning how to design and code your own website, but too busy to take classes during the day? Embark on October 10 for Le Wagon by Night - Short Trip to Front-End!

Journey description

A 3-hour class, every Monday during 10 weeks (from October 17 until December 19 - one week break during the autumn holidays).
Free info session & registrations on October 10 at 7pm!

Scheduled stops

Git, GitHub, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, UX/UI, web forms, CMS (WordPress,...), etc.

Final destination

A website designed, coded and deployed, from A to Z, by your own hands.
